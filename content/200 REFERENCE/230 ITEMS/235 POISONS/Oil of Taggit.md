- # Oil of Taggit

    - **Type:** Poisons
    - **Rarity:** uncommon
    - **Cost:** 400 gp
- **Property:** contact



## Description 

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 24 hours. The poisoned creature is unconscious. The creature wakes up if it takes damage.
