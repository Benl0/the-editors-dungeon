- # Malice

    - **Type:** Poisons
    - **Rarity:** uncommon
    - **Cost:** 250 gp
- **Property:** inhaled



## Description 

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 1 hour. The poisoned creature is blinded.
