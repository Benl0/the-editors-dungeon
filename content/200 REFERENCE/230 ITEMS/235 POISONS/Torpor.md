- # Torpor

    - **Type:** Poisons
    - **Rarity:** rare
    - **Cost:** 600 gp
- **Property:** ingested



## Description 

A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or become poisoned for 4d6 hours. The poisoned creature is incapacitated.
