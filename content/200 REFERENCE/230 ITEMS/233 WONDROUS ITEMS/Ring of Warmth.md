- # Ring of Warmth
- ![[ring.png]]
    - **Type:** Wondrous Items
    - **Rarity:** uncommon
    - **Cost:** 1000 gp
- **Property:** requires attunement



## Description 

While wearing this ring, you have resistance to cold damage. In addition, you and everything you wear and carry are unharmed by temperatures as low as -50 degrees Fahrenheit.
