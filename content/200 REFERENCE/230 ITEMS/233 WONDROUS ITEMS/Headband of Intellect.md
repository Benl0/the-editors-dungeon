- # Headband of Intellect

    - **Type:** Wondrous Items
    - **Rarity:** uncommon
    - **Cost:** 8000 gp
- **Property:** requires attunement



## Description 

Your Intelligence score is 19 while you wear this headband. It has no effect on you if your Intelligence is already 19 or higher.
