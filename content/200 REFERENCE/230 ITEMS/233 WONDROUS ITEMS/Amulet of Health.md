- # Amulet of Health

    - **Type:** Wondrous Items
    - **Rarity:** rare
    - **Cost:** 8000 gp
- **Property:** requires attunement



## Description 

Your Constitution score is 19 while you wear this amulet. It has no effect on you if your Constitution is already 19 or higher.
