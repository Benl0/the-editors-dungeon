- # Ring of Free Action
- ![[ring.png]]
    - **Type:** Wondrous Items
    - **Rarity:** rare
    - **Cost:** 20000 gp
- **Property:** requires attunement



## Description 

While you wear this ring, difficult terrain doesn't cost you extra movement. In addition, magic can neither reduce your speed nor cause you to be paralyzed or restrained.
