# Lesser Restoration
- ![[heal.png]]
- **Casting:**
    - **Casting Time:** 1 action
    - **Duration:** Instantaneous
    - **Range:** Touch

    - **Concentration:** No

- **Info:**
    - **Level:** 2nd
    - **School:** Abjuration
- **Components:**

## Description:
You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.
