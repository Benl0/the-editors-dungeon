# Create Bonfire
- ![[create-bonfire.png]]
- **Casting:**
    - **Casting Time:** 1 action
    - **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
    - **Range:** 60 feet
    - **Ritual:** No
    - **Concentration:** Yes
- **Classes:**
    - [[Wizard]]
    - [[Warlock]]
    - [[Sorcerer]]
    - [[Druid]]
    - [[Artificer]]

- **Info:**
    - **Level:** Cantrip
    - **School:** Conjuration
- **Components:**
    - VS


## Description:
You create a bonfire on ground that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, the bonfire fills a 5-foot cube. Any creature in the bonfire's space when you cast the spell must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d8 fire damage. A creature must also make the saving throw when it enters the bonfire's space for the first time on a turn or ends its turn there.\nThe spell's damage increases by 1d8 when you reach 5th level (2d8), 11th level (3d8), and 17th level (4d8).
