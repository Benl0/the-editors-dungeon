| **Silver**   | 10   | 1    | 1/20 | 1/10  | 1/100  |
| **Electrum** | 50   | 5    | 1    | 1/5   | 1/50   |
| **Gold**     | 100  | 10   | 2    | 1     | 1/10   |
| **Platinum** | 1000 | 100  | 20   | 10    | 1      |
