# Squire of Solamnia


## Prerequisite 
Dragonlance Campaign, Fighter or Paladin class or Knight of Solamnia background

## Description
Advantage on weapon attacks rolls and +1d8 to damage (PB/day), and 5 ft of movement to mount or dismount.
