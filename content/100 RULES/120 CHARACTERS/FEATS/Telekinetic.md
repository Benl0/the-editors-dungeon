# Telekinetic


## Prerequisite 
None

## Description
+1 in [[Abilities#Intelligence|Intelligence]], [[Abilities#Wisdom|Wisdom]], or [[Abilities#Charisma|Charisma]], you learn mage hand and you can try to telekinetically shove one creature (5 ft).
