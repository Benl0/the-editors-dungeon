# Telepathic


## Prequisite 
None

## Description
+1 in [[Abilities#Intelligence|Intelligence]], [[Abilities#Wisdom|Wisdom]], or [[Abilities#Charisma|Charisma]], you can cast detect thoughts and you can speak telepathically to any creature within 60 ft.
