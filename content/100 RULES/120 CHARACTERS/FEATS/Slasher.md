# Slasher


## Prequisite 
None

## Description
+1 in [[Abilities#Strength|Strength]] or [[Abilities#Dexterity|Dexterity]], reduce target's speed by 10 ft when you hit (slashing) and target has disadvantage on attacks rolls.
