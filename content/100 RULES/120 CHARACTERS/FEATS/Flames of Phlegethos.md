# Flames of Phlegethos


## Prequisite 
Tiefling

## Description
+1 in [[Abilities#Intelligence|Intelligence]] or [[Abilities#Charisma|Charisma]], reroll any 1 on fire spell damage, and cause flames to wreathe you if you cast a fire spell.
