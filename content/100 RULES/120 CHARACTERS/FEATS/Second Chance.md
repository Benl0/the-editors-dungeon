# Second Chance


## Prerequisite 
Halfling

## Description
+1 in [[Abilities#Dexterity|Dexterity]], [[Abilities#Constitution|Constitution]], or [[Abilities#Charisma|Charisma]], and you can force a creature to reroll its attack roll if it hits you.
