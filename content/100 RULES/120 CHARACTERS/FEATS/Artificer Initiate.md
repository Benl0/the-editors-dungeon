# Artificer Initiate


## Prerequisite 
None

## Description
You learn one cantrip and one 1st-level [Artificer](https://benl0.github.io/The-Editors-Dungeon/tags/DnD/Spells/Class/Artificer) spell (cast without slot), proficiency with one type of artisan's tools.
